Manali Trip Package

"Enchanting Manali: Mountains and Tranquility"

Day 1

Arrival in Manali: Upon arrival, be greeted by the majestic Himalayas as we transfer you to your cozy mountain retreat.

Day 2

Rohtang Pass Excursion: Embark on an exhilarating journey to Rohtang Pass (subject to weather conditions), where you'll be captivated by snow-capped peaks, glaciers, and breathtaking vistas.

Day 3

Sightseeing in Manali: Visit Hadimba Devi Temple, a serene wooden temple surrounded by tall cedar trees. Explore the tranquil Vashisht hot springs and the artistic charm of the Tibetan Monastery.
