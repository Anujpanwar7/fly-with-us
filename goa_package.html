Goa Trip Package

"Explore Goa's Coastal Charms"

Day 1

Arrival in Goa: Upon arrival, soak in the coastal breeze as we transport you to your beachfront accommodation.

Day 2

Historical Goa Tour: Discover Goa's rich history with visits to ancient churches and architectural marvels like the Basilica of Bom Jesus and Se Cathedral.

Day 3

Beach Bliss: Spend the day unwinding on Goa's renowned beaches. Choose between the lively shores of Baga Beach for water sports or the serene ambiance of Palolem Beach for relaxation.

Day 4

Culinary Delights: Participate in a cooking class to learn the secrets of authentic Goan cuisine and savor the dishes you create.

Day 5

Adventure & Nature: Engage in an adventure-filled day with optional activities like parasailing, jet-skiing, or trekking through Goa's lush hinterlands.

Day 6

Farewell Goa: Enjoy a relaxed morning, soaking in the final moments of the coastal paradise. Departure: Bid adieu to Goa with cherished memories as we transfer you to the airport for your onward journey.
