Rajasthan Trip Package

"Enchanting Manali: Mountains and Tranquility"

Day 1

Arrival in Jaipur: Begin your journey in the Pink City, where our team welcomes you and transfers you to your accommodation..

Day 2

Jaipur Sightseeing: Explore the grandeur of Amber Fort, taking an elephant or Jeep ride up to the hilltop fortress. Visit the City Palace, a blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture, and Jantar Mantar, an astronomical observatory

Day 3

Drive to Jodhpur: Travel to Jodhpur, the Blue City, known for its majestic Mehrangarh Fort towering over the city.

Day 4

Exploring Jodhpur: Discover the vibrant local markets, the serene Jaswant Thada, and the bustling Clock Tower.

Day 5

Journey to Udaipur: Travel to the City of Lakes, Udaipur, famed for its palaces, lakes, and romantic settings.

Day 6

Drive to Jodhpur: Travel to Jodhpur, the Blue City, known for its majestic Mehrangarh Fort towering over the city.
